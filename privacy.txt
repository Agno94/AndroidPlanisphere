Privacy Policy
Planisphere


This App can access your location. The location is used to calculate the star
chart.

Location information is not collected or stored on your device and is not shared
with anyone.

Display options and settings are the only information stored locally on your
device. No information does ever leave your device. This app does not connect to
the Internet at all.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact 1a.timescale@gmail.com.
