The App shows:

- over 9000 stars
- constellation lines, boundaries and names
- planets of the solar system
- azimuthal grid
- equatorial grid
- horizon
- ecliptic
- celestial equator

The App works on every place of the earth. An Internet connection is not required. The location can be entered manually or is provided by the device. There are several options to enable and disable elements of the chart, select the maximum apparent magnitude, choose the color theme and set the date and time.
